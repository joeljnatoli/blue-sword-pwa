How It Works

Modular. Scalable. Deployable.

A self-sustaining energy ecosystem built for portability and resilience — deploying wherever your hardware needs to be, grid-connected or completely remote.

Modular & portable

Containerised, transportable units deploy at any site with feedstock access — no permanent grid connection required. Ideal for rapid expansion or remote deployment.

Rapid deployment

Operational within weeks of site preparation rather than years — enabling aggressive growth timelines for AI and mining fleets, with feedstock partnerships accelerating local supply.

Global feedstock access

Over 300M tons of plastic and rubber waste are produced annually. We assist with local feedstock-sourcing partnerships — ensuring reliable supply chains in any geography.
